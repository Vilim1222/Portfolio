About

Born on February 26, 1999, Vilim began his creative journey in art school, where he developed a passion for visual arts. He furthered his education at the Film School in Kinoklub Zagreb, gaining valuable experience in film production. He later specialized in digital video and editing at Algebra, and also completed three years of design studies there. With over six years of experience in photography, he has developed a distinctive style, focusing on detail, aesthetics, and emotion in every shot. Motivated and ambitious, he constantly seeks new challenges and opportunities to grow in the world of creativity and visual arts..
